About GymSystems

Gymsystems is an open-source system for managing TeamGymnastics tournaments.

It covers every aspect of hosting tournaments, from clubs communicating their list of gymnasts, the disciplines they'll compete in and any allergies they might have so that the organizers can better plan the menu - to publishing the scores for each teams participation, both online and on big screen.

For information on how to use this system, if you want to host your own tournament, we keep a comprehensive user manual here
